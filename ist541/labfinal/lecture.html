Hook

A hook is a sentence that captures the readers' attention. It can be a famous quote, an interesting fact, or a question. It is always the first sentence.

Some examples include:

Interesting fact- Corals are actually colonies of microscopic polyps!
Famous quote- Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. once said, "I have a dream."
Question- When you think of corals, do you pictures rocks, or animals?


Thesis

A thesis is the roadmap to your entire essay. It tells the reader what to expect in each paragraph. In our class, our thesis must have three main points.
